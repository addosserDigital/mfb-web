Social Impact

We are deeply rooted in the communities we serve and are committed to making a difference to make the world a better place.  Through our various corporate social initiatives, we aim to create a positive impact, fostering sustainable development and improving the livelihoods of the communities we operate in.


Hence, our social impact framework is hinged on our 4E’s of Empowerment, Education, Environment and Engagement. These four tenets serve as our ethical compass towards achieving some of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) for developing countries by the United Nations.

4E’s

Engagement

We constantly engage people within the communities especially youths in the area of arts, sports and culture in-order to influence positive behavioral change.
