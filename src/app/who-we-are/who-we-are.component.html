About Addosser

Addosser Microfinance Bank is a leading Microfinance Bank in Nigeria that has been empowering businesses and individuals since its establishment in 2008. With a strong commitment to widening financial options for Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs), we offer a comprehensive range of value-added products and services to cater to their specific needs.

Over the past 15 years, our dedicated team of more than 300 professionals has disbursed repeat loans exceeding N65 billion, making a significant impact on the growth and success of our customers. As one of the established Microfinance banks in Nigeria, we are proud to serve over 137,000 customers across the nation.

At Addosser, we have structured our operations into three key business groups: Retail and Microenterprise Banking, SME Banking, and Specialized Products & Investments. Through these divisions, we provide tailored support to our customers, fostering strong relationships, and connecting them to a world of opportunities.
Our aim is to be acknowledged as a one stop shop for financial services pushing boundaries to increasing access to finance for MSMEs and individuals through our diverse business mix of Core banking, Fintech, and Insurance allowing us to offer a comprehensive suite of solutions to meet the evolving demands of our valued customers.

At Addosser Microfinance Bank Limited, we are driven by our mission to create value, build relationships, and make a positive difference in the lives of our customers and the communities we serve. We believe that financial inclusion is essential for economic growth and development. We are proud to play a role in helping MSMEs grow and thrive, and in improving the living conditions of everyday people.
